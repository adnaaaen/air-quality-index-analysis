What is AQI (Air Quality Index)?

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is a measure used to communicate how polluted the air currently is or how polluted it is forecast to become. It is designed to inform the public about air quality and its potential impact on health.

Categories
Good (0–50) Moderate (51–100) Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups (101–150) Unhealthy (151–200) Very Unhealthy (201–300) Hazardous (301–500)
